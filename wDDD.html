Team Lore

In the quirky town of Ding Dong, Texas, the "Ditchers" made a splash in the National Chicken Fighting League (NCFL), where Shoulder Wars unfolded in the refreshing waters of a pool. Nestled within the whimsical charm of Ding Dong, the Ditchers became a symbol of resilience and community spirit in the professional league. The team's base, surrounded by the playful atmosphere of the town, transformed into a gathering point where fans from Ding Dong and beyond, adorned in team colors, converged to witness the dynamic spectacle of aquatic competition.
