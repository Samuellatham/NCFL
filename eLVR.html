Team Info.

In the idyllic setting of Love Valley, North Carolina, the "Roses" bloomed as a captivating presence in the National Chicken Fighting League (NCFL), where Shoulder Wars unfolded in a refreshing pool. Nestled within the heart of Love Valley, the Roses became a symbol of beauty and tenacity in the professional league. The team's base, surrounded by the romantic allure of the town, transformed into a focal point where fans gathered, adorned in rose-themed attire, to witness the dynamic spectacle of aquatic competition.
