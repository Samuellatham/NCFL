Team Information

In the serene town of Yogaville, Virginia, the tranquil waters of the NCFL pool became the battleground for the "Instructors," a team blending mindfulness with the competitive fervor of Shoulder Wars. Nestled in Yogaville's peaceful surroundings, the Instructors brought a unique approach to the professional league, combining the art of yoga with the strategic challenges of chicken fighting. The Yogaville pool, surrounded by lush greenery and echoing with the sounds of tranquil meditation, transformed into an arena where the Instructors, donned in zen-inspired uniforms, displayed a harmonious fusion of athleticism and spiritual balance.

At the forefront of the Instructors' lineup were two standout players, Doak Dozier and Taylor Lauterbach, each bringing their distinct strengths to the team. Doak Dozier, a former college baseball player, seamlessly integrated the principles of balance and strength into his Shoulder Wars maneuvers. Taylor Lauterbach, a rising star in both the basketball and chicken-fighting communities, added a dynamic energy to the Instructors. With lightning-fast reactions and a fearless approach to competition, Lauterbach became known for pushing the boundaries of traditional Shoulder Wars strategy.

Together, Dozier and Lauterbach formed a formidable duo that embodied the ethos of Yogaville. The Instructors not only sought victory in the NCFL but also aimed to spread a message of mindfulness and unity through the unexpected medium of professional chicken fighting. The Yogaville community rallied behind their team, finding inspiration in the Instructors' unique fusion of athleticism and spiritual awareness within the spirited world of Shoulder Wars.
